Our Services

E-learning

Online / Instructional Design / iClicker Support

Using adult-education theory and proven instructional design methods, we design and build online courses to enhance the professional development of VCH staff and physicians. We support the learner experience through Learning Hub, which provides 24/7 access to courses from any computer at work or home, and track the individual learner data.

New Media

Engagement Tools / Apps & Mobile / AR & VR

From app development, to mobile, to strategic Employee Engagement initiatives, our Agile “manifesto” drives our passion for innovating thinking, facilitates working more collaboratively, and allows us to respond to the changing needs of our clients and VCH and continually striving for excellence.

Web

Course Shells / Team Sites / Design & Development

We utilize multiple web-based technologies to design interactive, responsive, e-learning course tramples, community-of-practice team sites, and other customized applications that are optimized to address the diverse needs of our clients across VCH and other BC health authorities.

Motion

Photography / Video Production / Animation

Our staff have expertise in the areas of visual animation, photography, audio recording, and sound editing. We can also manage every aspect of video production; from storyboarding, to set up, to filming, through to post production.

Print

Branding / Infographics / Visual Communications

We may begin with just a simple message, but in the hands of our talented designers, the message can become a brand, and infographic, a poster, or any other form of print or digital design communication.
